# 🏟️ AI Stadium Copilot - FIFA World Cup 2026

![AI Stadium Copilot Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x400)

## 🌍 Overview

AI Stadium Copilot is a Generative AI-powered smart stadium platform designed for FIFA World Cup 2026.

The solution improves the tournament experience for:

- ⚽ Fans
- 🏟 Stadium Operators
- 👥 Volunteers
- 🚨 Emergency Teams
- 🌱 Sustainability Teams

by providing AI-powered navigation, multilingual assistance, crowd intelligence, accessibility support, transportation guidance, and real-time operational insights.
